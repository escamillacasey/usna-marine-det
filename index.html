About the Detachment

The Marine cadre at the Naval Academy engages and educates all midshipmen on the mission and organization of the Marine Corps, its relationship with the Navy, and opportunities for service as Marine officers.

Through strategic communications and formal USMC training, the detachment recruits, selects, and prepares qualified midshipmen to commission as second lieutenants ready for The Basic School.
