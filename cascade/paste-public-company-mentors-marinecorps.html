Marine Company Mentors

One Marine officer dedicated to each of the Brigade's 36 companies.

What is a company Marine mentor?

A company Marine mentor is a Marine officer assigned to one midshipman company in Bancroft Hall. There are 36 companies in the Brigade and 36 company mentors — one for each. Your mentor lives and works on the Yard and is the detachment's primary, day-to-day link to mids in that company.

The mentor role is about presence and influence, not administration. Mentors extend the Marine Detachment's engagement model — informing, influencing, assessing, selecting, and equipping midshipmen for commissioning as Marine officers — at the individual and company level.

What mentors do

Company mentors help mids understand what it means to be a Marine officer, whether or not a midshipman intends to commission into the Marine Corps. Typical areas include:

  • Marine Corps culture, customs, and courtesies
  • The commissioning path from USNA (PROTRAMID, MAGTF, Leatherneck, selection)
  • Summer training preparation and expectations
  • Career and MOS conversations grounded in real fleet experience
  • Moral, mental, and physical leadership — modeled in the company environment

Mentors do not replace your company officer for discipline, accountability, or administrative matters. They complement the Commandant's Department by providing a consistent Marine voice in the company.

How to find your mentor

Your mentor is tied to your company, not your major or sport. If you are a 3/C in 14th Company, your mentor is the Marine assigned to 14th Company. Ask your chain of command in Bancroft if you are unsure who that is.

Current mentor assignments (photos, email, primary duty, and collateral assignments) are on this site after USNA login — not on the open internet. View mentor assignments (requires authentication on usna.edu/MarineCorps).

Mentor vs. other Marines on the Yard

Marines at USNA serve in several communities. Your company mentor is the right first stop for Marine-option guidance in your company. Other Marines you may meet serve different roles:

Community Relationship to you When to start here
Company Marine mentor One Marine per company; informs and influences mids in Bancroft Marine option questions, summer training prep, MOS conversations
MARDET Detachment staff (Sampson Hall) Selection policy, summer training administration, accessions
Company officer Leads your company day-to-day (Navy or Marine) Discipline, accountability, company leadership
Instructor / coach Academic or extracurricular billet Department or sport-specific mentorship
