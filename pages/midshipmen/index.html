Why Marines?

The Marine Corps exists to fight and win our Nation's wars by developing leaders capable of making sound decisions under pressure and leading Marines in the most demanding environments. At the Naval Academy, your journey begins with the opportunity to develop the character, judgment, and warfighting mindset required to earn the title Marine Officer.

That path aligns with the Academy's strategic vision: developing leaders of character and warfighters of consequence — preparing the next generation of officers for the privilege of leading sailors and Marines.
