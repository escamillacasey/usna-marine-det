Marine Company Mentors

One Marine officer dedicated to each of the Brigade's 36 companies.

The Marine Company Mentor program gives midshipmen a direct connection to the Marine Corps through a assigned officer in their company. Each mentor educates mids on customs, courtesies, career opportunities, summer training, and the path to selection.

Not sure how mentors fit among other Marines on the Yard? See Marines on the Yard for the full picture.
